Short Biography

I am a Ph.D. student at the Key Laboratory of Social Computing and Cognitive Intelligence and the National and Local Joint Engineering Laboratory of Computer Aided Design, Dalian University of Technology. I am under the supervision of Prof. Xiaopeng Wei and Prof. Xin Yang, with co-supervision from Bo Dong. In addition, I work closely with my lab colleagues Haiyang Mei and Wen Dong, as well as my best friends Hunan Chen (Tongji University) and Haisong Gong (Institute of Automation, CAS). During my undergraduate studies, I conducted research on swarm intelligence algorithms in the Jiangxi Key Laboratory of Intelligent Information System, under the guidance of Prof. Chunquan Li.

Research Interests

  • MLLM: e.g., Visual question answering, Multimodal instruction tuning and Aided visual reasoning.
  • Computer Vision: e.g., image/video understanding and generation, Object Counting, Material Reconstruction.
  • Evolutionary Computation: Swarm intelligence optimization algorithms such as Particle Swarm Optimization(PSO).
